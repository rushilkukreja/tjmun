Anmol Karan

Chair

Hey everyone! My name is Anmol Karan, and I am thrilled to be your Director of Specialized Agencies this year. I am a senior at TJ and have been participating in MUN for the past four years. From being forced to dance in committee to running around each committee with Candygrams and donations as Director of Hospitality last year, MUN has been one of my best experiences in high school. As an avid delegate myself, I understand the thrill and potential challenges involved in partaking in MUN conferences. But it’s helped me grow as a speaker and communicator, and I hope your experience at TechMUN helps you grow in the same way.

Outside of school, I am a huge fan of the Washington Commanders. I watch all their games and love talking about football to my friends and family. I like to play the bassoon as part of TJ’s band and (occasionally) orchestra and I love listening to music.

In committee, I’ll be looking for creative solutions to the unique problems that you face. Don’t be afraid to think outside the box and expand your knowledge of the topics. Above all, have fun, meet interesting people, and enjoy your time at TECHMUN XXXII.

Aayush Sethi

Chair

Hey guys! I’m Abhiram, a junior at TJ and I’m excited to be your other chair for this committee. I started doing MUN two years ago in freshman year, but in that amount of time, it has become one of my favorite clubs and a big part of my life at this school. MUN has impacted me in many ways, like helping me get better at public speaking and better at thinking on my feet , so I hope TechMUN and your future MUN experiences will impact you in the same way. Outside of MUN, I enjoy playing video games, reading books, and spending time with my friends and family, at least when I’m not studying for my next test.

In this committee, I will be looking for delegates who are diplomatic, respectful, and aren’t afraid to be creative. It is also important to me that I see delegates that are able to show how well they work with others while still staying in power. Last but not least, I hope that everyone in this committee learns from their experiences here and has fun while doing so. I’m looking forward to meeting all of you! If you have any questions or concerns about this committee, please feel free to reach out to us at nascartechmun2025@gmail.com.
