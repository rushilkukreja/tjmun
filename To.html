Aashka Doshi

Chair

Hi everyone! My name is Aashka, and I’m also a sophomore at TJ. Like Dev, this is my second year in MUN, and I’m so excited to be chairing this committee! I hope this conference will be a fun and immersive experience that leaves you with lasting memories and new friendships.

To me, MUN is more than just debate—it’s a space to learn, collaborate, and build connections with people who share similar interests, and I hope all of you can do the same with other delegates. In this committee, I hope to see strong diplomacy, teamwork, and of course, some spirited debate.

Outside of MUN, I love to dance, watch TV, and shop. And if you’re wondering about my go-to ice cream flavor, it’s Cookies and Cream! If you have any questions, send us an email at meltormergebenandjerrytechmun@gmail.com.

Dev Srivastava

Chair

Hi delegates! My name is Dev, and I am a sophomore at TJHSST. This is my second year in Model UN, and I am thrilled to be chairing this sweet committee. MUN has been an incredible experience for me, helping me sharpen my diplomacy, public speaking, and problem-solving skills, and I hope this conference will do the same for all of you. Throughout my MUN career, I’ve had the chance to work with a diverse range of people in many different committees, and I can confidently say that it has helped me grow both as a delegate and as a person.

Outside of MUN, I play football and wrestle for my school, and when I’m not busy with that, you’ll probably find me brainrotting on Instagram. As for the most important fact about me—my favorite ice cream flavor is Chocolate Chunk. If you have any questions, send us an email at meltormergebenandjerrytechmun@gmail.com.
